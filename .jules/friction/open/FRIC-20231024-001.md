## Problem
- Sentinel identified an environment poisoning vulnerability where `tokmd-cockpit` invokes `Command::new("cargo")` without unsetting ambient environment wrappers like `RUSTC_WRAPPER` or `RUSTC_WORKSPACE_WRAPPER`. However, `crates/tokmd-cockpit` is outside the allowed primary paths (`crates/tokmd-config`, `crates/tokmd-core`, `crates/tokmd`).
- As per the Sentinel instructions, since the strongest target was outside the shard, it should be recorded as a friction item instead of chasing it.

## Suggestion
- Add a centralized `cargo_cmd` factory (similar to `tokmd_git::git_cmd`) either inside `tokmd-cockpit` or in a shared crate to unset execution wrappers like `RUSTC_WRAPPER` and `RUSTC_WORKSPACE_WRAPPER`, and then update `crates/tokmd-cockpit/src/supply_chain.rs` and `crates/tokmd-cockpit/src/gates/contracts.rs` to use it. Care should be taken *not* to unset structural env vars like `CARGO_HOME` or `CARGO_TARGET_DIR` to avoid breaking CI environments.
