Scout: Found unnecessary Vec allocation in `redact_rows` in `tokmd-format` used during CSV/JSONL export.

## Target
`crates/tokmd-format/src/lib.rs`: `redact_rows` allocates a full Vec of FileRows even when only iterating.

## Proof
Structural: Replaced `Vec<FileRow>` allocation with a streaming iterator returning `Cow<'a, FileRow>`. This eliminates O(N) allocation for the container and O(N) clones when redaction is disabled (common case).
Verified via `cargo check` and `cargo test --workspace`.
